Welcome to JollofJobs

JollofJobs a leading recruitment agency for candidates and employers in the public and private sector. Our team of dedicated and experienced human resource professionals is committed to bring the best human resource solutions to you.

First we find out about the requirement of your organization and then set on looking for the perfect candidate to fill the vacancy quickly. First we look through our database to find the right candidate and in case our database falls short we run new recruiting programs.

Our strategy of complete evaluation of the candidate and analysis of the vacant position ensures choosing the right person which in turn reduces turnover, provides optimum productivity and boosts company morale. We provide placement solutions across verticals which include industrial construction, manufacturing, healthcare, engineering, mechanical, transportation, electrical, information technology and accounting
